# NutriFlow

## About NutriFlow

We are a technology company focused on improving everyday nutrition through intelligent food personalization. Most people struggle with maintaining a consistent healthy diet due to lack of time, knowledge, or motivation. This often leads to unhealthy eating patterns and long-term health issues.

We aim to solve this problem using an AI-driven nutrition system that continuously learns from user behavior. Users can define their goals, preferences, and dietary restrictions, and our AI will generate adaptive weekly meal plans that evolve over time based on real progress and feedback.

But we don’t stop there. We partner with local restaurants, healthy meal providers, and grocery services to offer flexible food options including ready-to-eat meals, meal kits, and smart grocery suggestions. Everything is packaged into a flexible subscription system that adapts to the user’s lifestyle rather than forcing a fixed plan.

### NutriFlow features

Adaptive meal plans: Your weekly nutrition plan evolves automatically based on your progress, energy levels, eating habits, and personal feedback. The system continuously learns from your behavior and adjusts recommendations to keep you moving toward your goals.

Smart nutrition scoring: Monitor the quality of your nutrition with an AI-powered health score that analyzes your meals and habits. Receive clear daily and weekly insights that help you understand your progress and make smarter decisions.

Flexible food options: Choose the approach that best fits your lifestyle, including delivered meals, meal kits, or personalized grocery recommendations. Switch between options anytime and enjoy complete flexibility without changing your nutrition goals.

Goal-based eating: Whether your objective is weight loss, muscle gain, long-term maintenance, or simply healthier eating habits, the system intelligently adjusts calories, meal recommendations, and nutrition priorities accordingly.

Habit tracking and insights: Discover patterns in your eating behavior with smart analytics that track consistency, meal timing, and nutrition trends. Receive actionable insights that help you build sustainable habits and maintain long-term progress.

Personalized recipe discovery: Explore thousands of recipes selected specifically for your preferences, dietary restrictions, and goals. The recommendation engine continuously introduces new meals to keep your nutrition plan enjoyable and prevent food fatigue.
